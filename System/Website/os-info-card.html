About

RQBBOX OS is a portable USB-based gaming operating system by RhysTech. It runs entirely from a USB drive without installation, emulation, or rebooting. Plug any USB drive, launch the server, and your gaming world is ready on any PC.

Slogans: "Plug Into Gaming." · "Portable Power Anywhere." · "Built For Gamers."

v2.0 USB Portable Open Source Chrome Extension Firefox Add-on XR Headsets
