RQBBOX OS is a portable USB-based gaming operating system developed by RhysTech. Runs entirely from a USB drive — no installation, no emulation, no reboot required. Features a PS5-inspired UI, pro synthesized audio engine, 43+ store packages, phone bootloader, and full Google Play Store integration.
