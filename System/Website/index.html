FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is RQBBOX a bootable operating system?▼
No. RQBBOX runs on top of your existing OS (Windows, macOS, Linux). No partitioning, no ISO, no boot config. Just plug in and launch like any app.
Does it support emulators or retro games?▼
No. RQBBOX is focused on native games, portable apps, and open-code development. We do not support emulators or retro console games.
What's the difference between Lite, Pro, and Creator?▼
Lite is the lightweight launcher with basic features. Pro adds cloud sync, advanced customization, and performance tools. Creator includes everything in Pro plus the SDK, plugin creator, and theme editor. All three are free.
Can I develop games and plugins for RQBBOX?▼
Yes. RQBBOX has an open-code system. You can build games, create plugins, design themes, and customize the UI. Supported languages: Rust, C++, JavaScript, Python. Visit our Developer Hub.
What platforms are supported?▼
Windows 10/11, macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon), Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora), ChromeOS, Android (Termux), iOS/iPadOS (PWA), Android TV, Apple TV — and any browser over LAN.
How does the Plugin Engine work?▼
Plugins are JavaScript/HTML packages placed in the Plugins folder on your USB. The engine loads them at startup. Themes modify CSS variables. Audio packs replace UI sounds. All open-code and community-driven.
Is my data secure?▼
Yes. All data stays on your USB drive. Profiles, saves, and settings are never sent to external servers unless you opt into cloud sync. Privacy mode disables all network access.
Is it really free?▼
Yes. RQBBOX OS is completely free. All editions, all features, all games and apps. No subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases. Always.
