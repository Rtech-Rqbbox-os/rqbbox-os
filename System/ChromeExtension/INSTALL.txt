HOW TO INSTALL RQBBOX OS CHROME EXTENSION
===========================================
(Works on google.com and googlechrome.com)

Step 1: Open Chrome and go to: chrome://extensions/

Step 2: Turn ON "Developer mode" (top-right toggle)

Step 3: Unzip the extension package to a folder:
   - Right-click rqbbox-os-chrome-extension.zip → Extract All
   - Or drag the folder somewhere like your Desktop

Step 4: Click "Load unpacked" button (top-left)
   - Select the folder you just extracted
   - The extension appears in your toolbar

Step 5: Go to google.com and search: RQBBOX OS
   - The info card appears on the right side or top of results
