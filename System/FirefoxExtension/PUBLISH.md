HOW TO PUBLISH FIREFOX EXTENSION (FREE - NO FEE)
================================================
Developer: rhyscotton20@gmail.com

Step 1: Create a Firefox Add-ons Developer Account
---------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/developers/
2. Sign in with any Firefox Account (create one free at accounts.firefox.com)
3. You're in — NO registration fee needed

Step 2: Submit the Extension
-----------------------------
1. Go to https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/developers/addon/submit/
2. Click "Submit a New Add-on"
3. Select "On your own" (not recommended — checking is fine)
4. Upload the ZIP file: rqbbox-os-firefox-extension.zip
5. Fill in the listing:
   - Name: RQBBOX OS — Google Search Info Card
   - Summary: Shows the RQBBOX OS info card on Google Search results
   - Description: Copy from store-listing.txt
   - Categories: "Web Development" or "Games & Entertainment"
   - License: MIT
6. Click "Submit"

Step 3: Wait for Review
------------------------
- Mozilla reviews within 1-2 business days
- Once approved, anyone can install from Firefox Add-ons store

Step 4: Share the Link
-----------------------
- https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/rqbbox-os/
- Add to your GitHub README, website, social media
