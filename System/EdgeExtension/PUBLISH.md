HOW TO PUBLISH MICROSOFT EDGE EXTENSION (FREE - NO FEE)
=======================================================
Developer: rhyscotton20@gmail.com

Step 1: Create a Microsoft Partner Center Account
---------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://partner.microsoft.com/en-us/dashboard/microsoftedge/
2. Sign in with any Microsoft account (create free at outlook.com)
3. Fill in developer profile — NO registration fee for Edge extensions!

Step 2: Submit the Extension
-----------------------------
1. In Partner Center, click "Create new" → "Extension"
2. Upload the ZIP: rqbbox-os-edge-extension.zip
3. Fill in the listing:
   - Name: RQBBOX OS — Google Search Info Card
   - Description: Shows the RQBBOX OS info card on Google Search results
   - Category: "Gaming" or "Developer Tools"
   - License: MIT
4. Click "Submit"

Step 3: Wait for Review
------------------------
- Microsoft reviews within 1-2 business days
- Once approved, anyone can install from Edge Add-ons store

Step 4: Share the Link
-----------------------
- https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/rqbbox-os
- Add to your GitHub README, website, social media
